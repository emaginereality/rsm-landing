The Story Behind RSM

Born from lived experience.

Karla Barnett Founder, Restoration Systems Mapping™

"I wasn't a clinician. I was a person in pain, searching for something that could actually reach the root of what I was carrying."

After walking through her own trauma, Karla found herself frustrated by tools that scratched the surface but never went deep enough. She wasn't looking for another coping strategy. She needed something that could see her — all of her — and help her understand why the same wounds kept reopening.

The vision for RSM came not from a textbook, but from a quiet moment of clarity she credits entirely to God. A framework that maps the whole person across six interconnected systems — because trauma doesn't live in just one place, and neither does healing.

RSM is built on that vision. It's for anyone who is done with surface-level solutions and ready to do the real work — gently, at their own pace, held with care.
